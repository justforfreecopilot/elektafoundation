Our Mission

{{}} {{}}

Our mission is to improve cancer care access in underserved regions and communities

We will do this by focusing on three focus areas which are

  • Training & Education
  • Cancer Care Infrastructure
  • Awareness & Prevention
{{}}

Half of all new cancer patients would benefit from RT treatment but 1 in 3 cancer patients don’t get the radiotherapy they need. 95% of all radiotherapy equipment is available to only 10% of the world’s population. We will address this by collaborating with academic institutions, governments and NGOs.
