Ride for Rwanda : 14th – 17th of September 2023

Our fundraising event Ride for Rwanda was sucessful!  Starting from the Crawley office in UK, the bike ride ended in Amsterdam, marking four days of effort, solidarity, and determination.  ​

    39 Incredible cyclists
    + 500 km in four days
    + US$ 55,000 funds raised
 

The funds from this event are being used for women living with advanced stage of cancer, by relieving physical and psychological suffering, as well as social distress.
