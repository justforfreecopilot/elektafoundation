Ride for Rwanda ​- 14th – 17th of September

Ride for Rwanda is our current fundraising event where Elekta colleagues are embarking on a cycle challenge to raise funds for the Elekta foundation. The funds will be used for women living with advanced stage of cancer, by relieving physical and psychological suffering, as well as social distress. They target to raise $50,000 which makes this “Ride4Rwanda” the largest fundraising event in Elekta’s history.  ​

What will they do?

A team of 40 colleagues from the UK and the Netherlands will cycle over 500 kms in four days! Each participant will raise a minimum of $1200 and the top fundraiser will be given the opportunity to visit the Elekta Foundation project in Rwanda and see, firsthand, how the funds are being used.
