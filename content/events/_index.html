New Elekta Foundation Academy is pleased to introduce Contouring and Plan Evaluation Workshop on Cervical Cancer

Saturday, 2 April, 2022
1:00pm-5:00pm TRT Turkey Time

The Elekta Foundation and the Turkish Society for Radiation Oncology (TROD) have partnered to deliver a hands-on contouring workshop for cervical cancer. Spots are limited to the first 30 registrants.

This workshop will offer a deeper understanding of treatment planning for cervical cancer. You will learn to contour the tumor and its bearing areas in a patient with cervical cancer; de-contour the Organs at Risk; and evaluate treatment plans to agree on the optimal plan for the patient.

The workshop is divided into three modules.

Module 1: Prework Training and Homework Assignment
Time Commitment Required: 90 minutes

Module 1 is a case study assignment that will be sent on 25 March, 2022 to all registrants. Training on ProKnow DS treatment planning platform will be provided and the assignment explained. Homework submission is mandatory to be eligible for the hands on workshop in Module 2. All registrants will have five days to work on Module 1 and submit homework to TROD by 29 March, 2022.

Module 2: Hands-on Workshop
Time Commitment Required: 240 minutes

Module 3: Post-workshop Assessment

All workshop attendees will have 24 hours after the workshop to revisit the homework assignment and deliver their assignment to TROD for evaluation.

Only registrants who complete all three modules successfully will be eligible to receive a Certificate of Participation by TROD.
